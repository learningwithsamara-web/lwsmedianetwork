Home Screen Access

Launch directly from your home screen like any other app — no browser needed.

Android

Chrome browser recommended

1

Open in Chrome

Visit lwsmedianetwork.com in your Chrome browser on Android.

2

Tap the Menu

Tap the three dots Menu in the top right corner of Chrome.

3

Add to Home Screen

Tap Add to Home screen from the menu options.

4

Confirm Install

Tap Add on the confirmation prompt. LWS Media Network will appear on your home screen instantly.

iPhone & iPad

Safari browser required

1

Open in Safari

Visit lwsmedianetwork.com in Safari on your iPhone or iPad. This will not work in Chrome on iOS.

2

Tap the Share Button

Tap the Share icon Share at the bottom of your Safari browser.

3

Add to Home Screen

Scroll down in the share menu and tap Add to Home Screen.

4

Confirm

Tap Add in the top right corner. LWS Media Network will appear on your home screen ready to use.
